--- title: Nothing layout: poem ---
(From Puerto del Sol Vol. 54.1 & 2 - The Absence Issue & The Korean Women Writers Issue | Spring 2019

Nothing

Says the cigarette deleting itself
through the window fan.

Says God, a toss of blue flowers
asleep in its mouth.

Ever look up at the stars at night and wonder
if somewhere else we’re already dead?

I’m not crying,
I just looked at the prank boutonnière for too long.

My breath feels like a pulse of numbers
on a long light’s crosswalk counting down and

I’m afraid the rain gathering in tire impressions along
Dead Senator Avenue also means nothing.

Nothing is the silence in the mouth
of the storm drain down the street.

Nothing is a song I only know the words to
when others are singing along.

—from Puerto del Sol Vol. 54.1 & 2 - The Absence Issue & The Korean Women Writers Issue | Spring 2019
